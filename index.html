Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2025

Summary: My Shopping List does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your shopping lists are stored locally on your device.

Information Collection and Use

My Shopping List is designed with your privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT require an account or registration
  • Does NOT track your usage or behavior
  • Does NOT use analytics or advertising services
  • Does NOT access the internet (no network permissions)

Data Storage

All your shopping lists and items are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is NOT sent to any servers
  • Is NOT shared with third parties
  • Will be deleted if you uninstall the app

Permissions

The app requires zero permissions. It does not access:

  • Internet/Network
  • Camera
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Storage (beyond app-specific storage)

Sharing Features

When you use the "Share" feature to send your shopping list:

  • Data is shared only through your chosen app (WhatsApp, Email, etc.)
  • Sharing is initiated by you and controlled by the Android system
  • No data is sent to our servers or third parties

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
